Extraction
on solana · player guide v0.3
Deposit from your Phantom wallet to top up your in-game $EXT, enter the dungeon, and hunt for the Excalibur Stone. Mine it and pull the 1% jackpot to win the entire prize pool in one hit. Refine the ore you haul out into $EXT, while your idle miner earns more around the clock. Withdraw your balance back to your Solana wallet anytime.
The Loop
1
Deposit & Enter the Dungeon
Top up your in-game $EXT from your Phantom wallet (USDC for now — $10 = 100,000 $EXT). Choose Free (no cost, limited runs) or a paid entry (1K–100K $EXT). Every paid entry splits 10% burn · 10% dev · 80% into the jackpot prize pool. Navigate 10 procedural floors, mine ore, fight enemies with E.
2
Explore Special Floors
Vein Floors glow with a "✦ VEIN FLOOR ✦" banner — every ore node gives 2× GC. Healing Shrines restore +60 HP when you press E nearby. Chests drop pickaxes.
3
Defeat Guardian Bosses
Floor 5 spawns the Guardian (600 HP) and Floor 10 the Inferno Guardian (1200 HP). Both fire 3-shot fireball volleys toward you with a ±fan spread. Stay mobile.
4
2× Loot on Floors 6–10
All GC mined on floors 6–10 is automatically doubled. Vein Floors stack on top of this for up to 4× GC.
5
Hunt the Excalibur Stone ⚔
One ⚔ Excalibur Stone is hidden per paid run — spawns per floor: 3% on floor 3, 4% on floor 4, 5% on floors 5+. Mine it and roll the 1% jackpot pull. Win and you receive the entire prize pool sent to your Solana wallet. The pool then resets. ★ 5% secondary chance to also receive the ultra-rare Mythic Prismatic Pickaxe — the game's rarest item, unobtainable any other way.
6
Equip & Mine Idle
Equip your best pickaxe in Backpack. Your miner auto-swings 24/7 against the crystal cave, earning $EXT. Rare rocks give up to 25× payout.
7
Reforge at the Blacksmith
Spend $EXT at the Blacksmith tab to re-roll a pickaxe's stats. Select a pickaxe from the grid, then hit Re-roll — you see Before vs After before committing. Costs scale with rarity.
8
Withdraw Your $EXT
Your in-game $EXT balance is real. Request a withdrawal from the Home screen and it will be sent to your connected Solana wallet within 24 hours. Minimum withdrawal: 50,000 $EXT. Your balance carries over between sessions — never expires.
Dungeon Guide
Risky Mode has 1.5× more spikes & mobs and multiplies final GC by 1.5×. Fall = instant death. Double jump supported. Kill bonus: +8 GC (floors 1–5), +20 GC (floors 6–10).
✦ Vein Floor — 10% chance per floor. Glowing crystal banner announces it. All ore nodes on that floor give 2× GC. Stacks with 2× loot bonus on floors 6–10.
🩸 Healing Shrine — 10% chance per floor. A blood chalice glows on a platform. Press E nearby to restore +60 HP (capped at 100). One use per shrine.
⚔ Excalibur Stone — Spawn chance: 3% on floor 3, 4% on floor 4, 5% on floor 5+. Mine it to roll the 1% jackpot pull. Win = entire prize pool sent to your wallet. The pool resets after every win. ★ Hidden 5% chance: the jackpot also awards the ultra-rare Mythic Prismatic Pickaxe — the only way to obtain the Mythic tier.
🎵 Cave Music — Procedural dungeon music plays automatically. Toggle with the 🎵 button in the HUD. Sub-bass drones, chord pads, and ethereal highs create an immersive cave atmosphere.
❄ Falling Stalactites — From floor 4 onward, glassy spikes drop from the ceiling at random points across the cavern (a new one every 1–8s). They shatter on the ground, or deal 15 damage if one lands on you. Keep moving.

Ore Refinery
At the end of any paid run (free runs don't qualify), refine the ore you hauled out into real $EXT. Each ore has a refine value (see the ore table); your haul's total refine value sets your payout as a multiple of your entry fee.
Payout: entry fee × (haul refine-value ÷ 364), clamped between 0.22× (floor — any run with at least 1 rock mined) and 1.65× (cap). A haul worth ~364 refines for roughly your entry back; ~600 hits the cap. Bigger entries refine into proportionally more $EXT.
Death tax: If you choose to descend deeper and then die, you lose 30% of that run's GC and 30% of its ore haul before refining. Cash out safe, or push your luck for a bigger haul.
Deposit & Withdraw
Deposit: Tap Deposit in the top bar — your connected Phantom wallet signs the transfer and your in-game $EXT is credited instantly. Deposits are in USDC for now at $10 = 100,000 $EXT (10,000 $EXT per $1). Once the $EXT token launches, you'll deposit $EXT directly, 1:1.
Withdraw: Request a withdrawal from Settings — your $EXT is sent to your connected Solana wallet within 24 hours. Minimum withdrawal: 50,000 $EXT. Your balance carries between sessions and never expires.
$EXT & Economy
Entry Fee Slider: Set your $EXT entry (1K–100K) on a log scale. Higher entry = better loot multiplier (up to 20×) + higher Excalibur Stone spawn odds. Every paid entry splits 10% burned · 10% to dev · 80% into the prize pool.
Loot Tiers: LOW (1K–3K $EXT) · MEDIUM (3K–10K) · HIGH (10K–50K) · MAX (50K–100K). The label shows on the dungeon entry screen.
Prize Pool: Displayed live on the Home screen (and in the jackpot jar on the Idle Miner). Grows with every paid dungeon entry — 80% of each fee. One winner takes the entire pool via the Excalibur Stone jackpot pull (1% chance). The pool resets to zero after each win and starts building again.
Save System: All progress (GC, $EXT, ore, upgrades, inventory) saves automatically — cloud-synced to your X (Twitter) account when signed in, so it follows you across devices. Settings → Reset wipes your local save.
